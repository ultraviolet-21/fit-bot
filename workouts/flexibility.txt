**Mobility & Stretching Flow**

**Time**: 30 mins
**Equipment**: Mat

**Flow-Based Routine (no repeats):**

* Cat-cow for 1 min
* Child’s pose for 1 min
* Thread-the-needle stretch – 1 min per side
* World’s greatest stretch – 1 min per side
* Hip 90/90 switches for 1 min
* Pigeon pose - 1 min per side
* Seated forward fold for 1 min
* Butterfly stretch for 1 min
* Supine spinal twist – 1 min per side
* Happy baby for 1 min
* Deep breathing in Savasana for 5 mins
