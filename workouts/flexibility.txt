Mobility & Stretching Flow

Time: 20 mins
Equipment: Mat

Flow-Based Routine (no repeats):

* Cat-cow for 1 min
* Child’s pose for 1 min
* Thread-the-needle stretch for 1 min per side
* World’s greatest stretch for 1 min per side
* Hip 90/90 switches for 1 min
* Pigeon pose for 1 min per side
* Seated forward fold for 90 sec
* Butterfly stretch for 90 sec
* Supine spinal twist for 1 min per side
* Happy baby for 1 min
* Deep breathing in Savasana for 5 mins
