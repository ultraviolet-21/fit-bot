**Pilates Flow**

**Time**: 30 mins
**Equipment**: Mat

**Warm-Up (5 mins)**

* Seated breathing and pelvic tilts
* Spine stretch forward
* Shoulder rolls

**Workout (20 mins)**

1. Hundred 
2. Single leg stretch 
3. Double leg stretch 
4. Criss-cross 
5. Rolling like a ball 
6. Leg circles 
7. Bridge with pulses 
8. Swimming (superman kicks) 
9. Side leg lifts 
10. Saw twist 

**Cool Down (5 mins)**

* Spine twist – 1 min
* Forward fold – 1 min
* Seated hip opener – 1 min per side
* Diaphragmatic breathing – 1 min
