Full-Body Strength (Bodyweight)

Time: 30 mins
Equipment: Optional dumbbells

Warm-Up (5 mins)

* Arm swings – 1 min
* Leg swings – 1 min
* Jumping jacks – 1 min
* Squats – 1 min
* Inchworm walkouts – 1 min

Workout (20 mins)
3 rounds:

1. Squats × 15
2. Push-ups × 10–15
3. Lunges × 10 each leg
4. Plank shoulder taps × 30 sec
5. Glute bridges × 20
6. Superman hold × 30 sec min

Cool Down (5 mins)

* Standing hamstring stretch
* Lunge with a twist
* Shoulder and arm stretch
* Forward fold
