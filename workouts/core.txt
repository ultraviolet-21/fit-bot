Core Focus

Time: 30 mins
Equipment: Mat
Format: 3 circuits × 2 rounds

Warm-Up (5 mins)

* Cat-cow for 1 min
* Bird dog for 1 min
* Standing twists for 2 mins
* Plank hold for 1 min

Workout (20 mins)

Circuit 1:
* Plank (hold for 45 sec)
* Bicycle crunches (30 sec)
* Flutter kicks (30 sec)

Circuit 2:
* Russian twists (30 sec)
* Leg raises (30 sec)
* Mountain climbers (30 sec)

Circuit 3:
* V-ups (30 sec)
* Side plank (30 sec each side)
* Dead bug (30 sec)

Cool Down (5 mins)

* Cobra stretch for 1 min
* Child’s pose for 1 min
* Supine twist for 1 min per side
* Deep breathing for 1 min
